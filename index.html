Plans

Each Peliscalidad plan determines the number of devices on which you can watch Peliscalidad content at the same time, and the amount of content will vary according to the plan.

Quality

With Peliscalidad you can watch movies and series up to ultra high definition quality (UHD), however, you may prefer to watch in standard definition (SD), high definition (HD) or ultra high definition (UHD).

Silver

  • 100 movies and 200 series
  • Maximum quality (HD)
  • You can only watch on one device

Gold

  • 400 movies and 500 series
  • Maximum quality (QHD)
  • You can only watch on 3 devices at a time

Platinum

  • Unlimited movies and series
  • Maximum quality (UHD)
  • You can only watch on 5 devices at a time
